# Safety And Compliance

## Highest-Risk Areas

- aggressive messaging
- voice cloning and likeness
- telephony consent
- injury-related coaching

## Minimum Rules For v1

- require explicit opt-in for SMS and calls
- expose quiet hours and escalation tolerance clearly
- keep emergency-contact features out of scope
- maintain a swappable inspired-by persona fallback
- preserve system-level copy constraints above persona flavor
